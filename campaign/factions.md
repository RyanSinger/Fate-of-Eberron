# Faction State

Factions tracked here with current agendas and threat levels. Base faction details are in the [GM's Guide](../gm-guide.md).

## Active Factions

*Factions become active as they enter play. Move them here from the reference list below.*

## Reference

### Dragonmarked Houses
- **House Cannith**: Fragmented into three branches, competing to reunify
- **House Deneith**: Expanding mercenary contracts, filling the power vacuum
- **House Medani**: Investigating threats others miss
- **House Phiarlan / Thuranni**: Shadow war between the split houses

### Criminal Organizations
- **Boromar Clan**: Halfling crime syndicate, controls gambling and smuggling in Sharn
- **Daask**: Monster-run organization pushing into Boromar territory
- **House Tarkanan**: Aberrant dragonmark bearers, assassins and outcasts

### National Powers
- **Breland**: Hosts Sharn, King Boranel aging, succession uncertain
- **The King's Dark Lanterns**: Breland's secret service, still active post-war

### Secret Powers
- **The Aurum**: Wealthy conspiracy seeking to control Khorvaire through gold
- **The Chamber**: Dragons manipulating events per the Draconic Prophecy
- **Lords of Dust**: Rakshasa serving imprisoned overlords
- **Dreaming Dark**: Quori manipulating mortals through dreams
- **Emerald Claw**: Militant Blood of Vol faction, officially disbanded
